Nick Wilton

Award winning English comedian, actor and scriptwriter

Nicholas Wilton is an English actor and scriptwriter. He has appeared as Mr Lister in the BBC soap opera EastEnders as a recurring character; he has also appeared in Carrott's Lib, Fast Forward and Jackanory.

Wilton studied English and Drama at the University of Kent.[1] His first professional engagement was in 1979 as a bluecoat entertainer at a Pontins holiday camp, he then spent two years as a stage manager in theatres and then moved onto to acting.
